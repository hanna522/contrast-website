Glaucoma

What is glaucoma?

Glaucoma is a group of eye conditions that damage the optic nerve. This damage is often caused by abnormally high pressure in your eye.

How to recognize?

Open-angle glaucoma

  • Loss of peripheral vision
  • Tunnel vision

Angle-closure glaucoma

  • Headache
  • Eye pain and redness
  • Nausea and vomiting
  • Blurred vision

How to take care?

  • Medication Adherence
  • Regular Check-ups
  • Protect Your Eyes
  • Healthy Lifestyle
  • Know Your Family History
